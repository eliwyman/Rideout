Based in Nanaimo, BC, Rideout Communications Inc. offers advertising solutions on high definition digital screens in public locations. Rideout Communications publishes Whats On Digest Magazine Nanaimo and www.whatsondigest.com (Nanaimo). Whats On Digest and www.whatsondigest.com are Vancouver Island’s largest and fastest growing activities and events magazine and Website. The Rideout Communications digital advertising screens are networked and strategically located to capture the attention and interest of people as they move through their daily activities. Whether in high traffic restaurants, fitness centers, or medical facilities, Rideout screens get noticed.

Unlike traditional signs, Rideout screens are dynamic and leverage the impact of television while rewarding viewers with calming and visually stimulating imagery. Rideout Communications sells advertising space to local businesses to be displayed in print and on screens in high traffic locations. In some cases the screens will be reaching a healthy cross section of society. In others the screens are located to target a specific demographic such as socioeconomic status, gender, or age. This ensures that your message is on screens situated in locations well-attended by your customers and prospects.

All advertising works to generate a specific behavior (buying) through the process Attention, Interest, Desire, Action. There is no better way to get a person's attention than via visual imagery. Once the attention is there we move through the steps culminating in need fulfillment.


Rideout Communications is a marketing company built on an understanding of human behavior, motivation and psychology. These principles are applied to how we approach the business of moving people from attention and interest in your business to desire for your products or services.


With Rideout Communications you have a custom and professionally developed presentation that runs in a loop all year long. People may see your message a few times per visit or 20 times but one thing is for sure – they will see your message! The smartest advertising is that which not only repeats but also appears across mediums. Rideout Communications offers the power of print, the reach of the internet, and the impact of TV. So why choose only one when you can have all three?


Advertising Works!


Successful advertising rarely succeeds through argument or calls to action. Instead, it creates positive memories and feelings that influence our behavior over time to encourage us to buy something at a later date. No one likes to think that they are easily influenced. In fact, there is plenty of evidence to suggest that we respond negatively to naked attempts at persuasion. Instead, the best advertisements are ingenious at leaving impressions.
